# vibe-code-too
We are going to have 4 main classes. the vehicle class, which will be responsible for the cars passing by the radar. Will cover the varying speeds, car type, and hat cars has passed. The radar class will cover the statistics regarding the cars passing. Will track speed of the car, whether or not the car is speeding, how many cars have passed per minute, as well as how many tickets the "officer" has given and how many more they have to issue to reach the quota. There will be a background class to control the image of the road, give visuals to the speed limit as well as the drawings of the cars coming by. The Ticket class will allow for the user to control/hand out tickets when the radar shows the car speeding. Their goal is to hit the ticket quota given on screen.
